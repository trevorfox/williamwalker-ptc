William Walker PTC

Programs & Events

Every program on this page is funded by families and run by volunteers — here's what the PTC makes happen at Walker, and how you can help.

Programs & Enrichment

What the PTC funds.

F

Field Trips

Buses, admission, and experiences beyond the classroom — the PTC helps make field trips possible for every student, every year.

Learn more →
A

Art Literacy

Volunteer-led art lessons that bring famous artists and hands-on projects into every classroom, several times a year.

Learn more →
M

Mystery Science

Hands-on science lessons that turn "why?" into experiments in every classroom.

O

Oregon Battle of the Books

Oregon's statewide reading competition — Walker teams read, quiz, and battle their way through a great book list.

R

Reading Week

A whole week celebrating books, with themed days, guest readers, and every student reading for the love of it.

F

Field Day

The classic end-of-year day of games, relays, and recess-level joy — equipment and supplies funded by the PTC.

S

SPED Track & Field Day

A district track and field day for students in specialized programs — the PTC helps Walker's athletes get there and shine.

T

Teacher Discretionary Funds

Every teacher gets PTC funds to spend on what their classroom actually needs — no paperwork, no waiting.

S

SEL Fund

Supporting social-emotional learning at Walker — resources that help students manage feelings, build friendships, and thrive.

P

PTC Grant Program

Staff can apply for PTC grants to fund big ideas — special projects, classroom tools, and new opportunities for students.

K

Kinder Pumpkin Patch

Kindergartners' first field trip — a fall visit to the pumpkin patch, funded by the PTC.

S

Staff Appreciation

Meals, treats, and thank-yous throughout the year for the teachers and staff who show up for our kids every day.

S

Staff PD Expenses

Helping Walker's teachers grow — the PTC chips in on professional development costs.

K

Kindness Fund

Quiet, immediate help for Walker families in need — coats, shoes, groceries, whatever a hard week calls for.

Events

What the PTC hosts.

W

Walkerthon

Our signature fall fundraiser — the whole school runs, laps get counted, and the community rallies behind our Wildcats.

Learn more →
B

Back to School Night

We're there to welcome families, answer questions, and sign up new volunteers.

T

Trunk or Treat

A safe, festive fall evening of decorated trunks and treats for our families.

M

Multicultural Night

A beloved tradition celebrating the rich diversity of the William Walker community.

E

End-of-Year & 5th Grade Parties

Celebrating a great year — and sending our 5th graders off in style.
